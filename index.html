The Academy Profession Degree Programme in Multimedia Design & Communication gives the graduates the right to use the title AP Graduate in Multimedia Design & Communication. The programme is an academy profession programme corresponding to 120 ECTS credits.
The objective of the programme is to provide graduates with the qualifications needed to independently design, plan, realise and manage multimedia tasks and to assist in the implementation, administration and maintenance of multimedia productions.
